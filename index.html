About Me

Hi! My name is Cecilia. I'm 23 and I graduated from Indiana University with a B.S. in biology in 2016. After a move to Austin, Texas, I got inspired by the potential in the tech industry and decided to make a change. I'm currently a student in UT Austin's Coding Bootcamp, learning all sorts of cool things, like how to use HTML and CSS to make this portfolio!

I currently do Neuroscience research at UT studying the relationships between stress, exercise, and addiction. Spoiler alert: exercise is really, really good for you and you should do it, even if you're a rat. When I'm not watching dopamine neurons fire, I like to explore Austin, hike, camp, travel, and I powerlift! I can deadlift 255lb, squat 230, and bench 145 for a 630lb total. Hopefully one day soon my web development skills will be as strong as my quads.
